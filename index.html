About EPC Ventures

Leading
Energy
Infrastructure

EPC Ventures is a leading energy infrastructure solutions provider based in Nagpur, Maharashtra. We specialize in the comprehensive execution of transmission and monopole projects, delivering innovative and sustainable infrastructure solutions for India's evolving energy sector.

With expertise spanning designing, consultancy, supply, and project execution, we offer end-to-end turnkey solutions that prioritize precision, safety, and efficiency. Our seasoned professionals integrate advanced technology with deep industry experience.

We have executed complex projects across Maharashtra, Karnataka, Telangana and Mumbai — working under MSETCL and KPTCL supervision for major real estate and utility clients.

What We Do

Our
Services

From structural concept design to final commissioning, EPC Ventures manages every phase of your energy infrastructure project with precision and accountability.

01
Designing & Consultancy
Innovative structural design of monopoles and towers engineered for optimal load-bearing capacity and long-term reliability.
  • Monopole & tower structural design
  • Load testing consultancy (CPRI)
  • Foundation design (caisson & conventional)
  • Optimization for site-specific conditions
02
Supply & Procurement
Superior monopoles, HT/LT overhead line materials, insulators, conductors, cable trays, and tower hardware — delivered on time.
  • Monopoles & structural sections
  • HT/LT overhead line materials
  • Insulators, conductors & accessories
  • Cable trays & tower hardware
03
Civil & Structural Works
Tailored foundation construction and precision erection & stringing services committed to safety and regulatory compliance.
  • Caisson & conventional foundations
  • Monopole & tower erection
  • Conductor stringing & termination
  • Height raising & road crossings
04
Project Management
End-to-end turnkey project management from conceptualization to commissioning — on time, on budget, every time.
  • Turnkey project execution
  • Testing & commissioning
  • Outage planning & execution
  • Client-centric reporting & transparency
