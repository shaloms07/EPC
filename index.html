About EPC Ventures

Leading
Energy

EPC Ventures is a leading energy infrastructure solutions provider based in Nagpur, Maharashtra. We specialize in comprehensive execution of transmission and monopole projects, delivering innovative and sustainable solutions for India's evolving energy sector.

With expertise spanning designing, consultancy, supply, and project execution, we offer end-to-end turnkey solutions that prioritize precision, safety, and efficiency — integrating advanced technology with deep industry experience across MSETCL and KPTCL supervised projects.

We have successfully executed complex projects across Maharashtra, Karnataka, Telangana, and the Mumbai region — trusted by both state utilities and major real estate developers.

What We Do

Our Services

From structural concept design to final commissioning, EPC Ventures manages every phase of your energy infrastructure project with precision and total accountability.

01
Designing & Consultancy
Innovative structural design of monopoles and towers engineered for optimal load-bearing capacity and long-term reliability in any site condition.
  • Monopole & tower structural design
  • CPRI load testing consultancy
  • Foundation design (caisson & conventional)
  • Optimization for site-specific conditions
02
Supply & Procurement
Superior monopoles, HT/LT overhead line materials, insulators, conductors, cable trays and tower hardware — sourced to the highest standards.
  • Monopoles & structural steel sections
  • HT/LT overhead line materials
  • Insulators, conductors & accessories
  • Cable trays & tower hardware
03
Civil & Structural Works
Tailored foundation construction and precision erection & stringing services — with deep expertise in space-constrained urban environments.
  • Caisson & conventional foundations
  • Monopole & tower erection
  • Conductor stringing & termination
  • Height raising & road crossings
04
Project Management
End-to-end turnkey project management from concept to commissioning. Rigorous testing, outage planning, and client-centric reporting every step of the way.
  • Turnkey project execution
  • Testing & commissioning
  • Outage planning & execution
  • Client-centric progress reporting
